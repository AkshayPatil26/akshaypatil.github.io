About Me

Hello, I’m Akshay Patil.

In 2017, I graduated from the New Jersey Institute of Technology (NJIT) with a M.S. in Computer Science.
I’ve been working as a Developer ever since.

In the final semester at NJIT, I took a User Experience Design class. I felt an instant connection with the subject.
I aspire to use my technical knowledge of computers and thoughtfully build Human Centered Interfaces for digital devices and beyond.

I am currently enrolled in Google’s UX Design Professional Certificate Course on Coursera.
My goal is to condition myself as a UX engineer in the coming months.
